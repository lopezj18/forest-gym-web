Our Services

At Forest Fitness we offer services for everyone. We have anything from yoga to weight lifting. We also offer individual personal training for thoes that would like to get the most from our workouts. Our services are listed below, click on a service to find out more infromation.

Yoga

This yoga class is designed to provide you with a challenging physical-cardio workout. The benefits of this practice will also help you to reduce stress, relax your mind, and improve your mood.

Zumba!

Dance your way into fitness! Combines Latin and international music with popular Latin and international dances to create an exhilarating, high-energy and calorie-burning dance fitness party that makes for a fun and effective workout.

Weight Lifting

We provide the best weight lifiting equipment for all body types and experiences. We have trained spotter professionals to guide you to get the best workout possible.

Pilates

Each Pilates session is personally tailored to meet your individual goals. As your goals and strength change, your Pilates workout changes accordingly.
